Medium

Languages:

GameMaker Studio, GameMaker language

Summary:

You are a medium. A bridge between the physical and ethereal worlds. Use your ghost-like abilities to possess animals, solve puzzles and defeat bosses, all while uncovering bits of a dark past that transcends life itself (or something).

Details:

A game programmed with the VGDev Club at Georgia Tech. I personally worked on programming, quality assurance, and level design. The programming primarily consisted of interactions between the environment and the player, such as the water and the wind. I also added the dialog system using GameMaker Language. I also received the Deleonic Award for my work on this game from VGDev.
