Who2020

Languages:

HTML, CSS, Javascript, D3

Summary:

A political quiz that shows you how similar you are to various candidates for president in 2020

Details:

The website includes a ranking showing overall similarity, as well as similarity by category. Unlike other quizzes such as iSideWith, you can pick multiple positions that are acceptable to you, and all candidates have answers for each question, which prevents a bias caused by number of questions which candidates have a position for.
